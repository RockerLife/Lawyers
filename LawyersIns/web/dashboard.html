Engagement Letter Templates
Customers Please Note: These Engagement Letter Templates are offered for your use to assist in meeting professional standards requirements for "establishing an understanding" with a client and to memorialize other provisions relating to the work to be performed. These Engagement Letter Templates are not intended to be used "as is". Like all documents which evidence agreement between parties, these documents are intended to be binding upon all the parties. As such, local law must be applied. Customers are encouraged to obtain legal advice from local counsel in drafting documents to be used locally in compliance with local law. There are 54 different jurisdictions in the United States, these documents are drafted generically without considering local jurisdictional differences. Specific jurisdictional licensing, case law and statutes should be considered by obtaining legal advice from an attorney in the user's local jurisdiction.
